Refund & Cancellation

Last updated: April 30, 2026

At Vandan, we value the trust you place in us. Please read this policy carefully before making any payments for our services.

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only considered in the following circumstances:

  • Double payment for the same transaction.
  • Technical failure where money was deducted but the Booking or Contribution was not recorded in our system.
  • Incorrect amount deducted due to a system glitch.

2. Non-Refundable Items

Please note that the following are strictly non-refundable:

  • Advance Payments: Advance payments made for booking a group are non-refundable once the booking is confirmed and the group has blocked the date for you.
  • Contributions: Any voluntary contributions made towards a group or cause are non-refundable.
  • Payment Gateway Fees: The transaction fees charged by our payment partner (approx. 2-3%) are not returned by the gateway and will be deducted from any eligible refund.
  • Missed Events: No refunds will be issued if the booked event date has passed.

3. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your booking or group profile at any time. However:

  • Cancellations will take effect immediately.
  • No partial refunds are provided for any unused portion of the service or for early cancellations.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team via the Contact Center within 48 hours of the transaction. You must provide your Transaction ID and Mobile Number.

Approved refunds will be processed within 5 to 7 working days and will be credited back to the original payment source (Bank/UPI/Card).

By using Vandan, you agree to these terms. We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time.
